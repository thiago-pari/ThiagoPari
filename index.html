Econometric Analysis of Labor Force Participation and Teaching Evaluations in R

This project focuses on the labor force participation of married women. Using the MROZ dataset, I employed linear, Probit, and Logit models to estimate labor force participation based on covariates such as income, education, experience, age, and presence of children. Additionally, I predicted labor force participation probabilities and examined the effects of different factors on these probabilities.
